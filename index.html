HealthLens: Innovations in Healthcare Access, Diagnosis, and Treatment

About HealthLens

HealthLens is a student project aimed at exploring innovations in healthcare delivery. Our project focuses on addressing key challenges in healthcare access, diagnosis accuracy, and treatment effectiveness through the use of advanced technologies and collaborative approaches.

Project Objectives

  • Enhancing access to healthcare services
  • Improving accuracy in disease diagnosis
  • Developing personalized treatment approaches
  • Empowering patients through education and engagement

Key Features

  • Telemedicine services for remote healthcare access
  • AI-driven diagnostic tools for early disease detection
  • Genomic testing for personalized treatment recommendations
  • Health literacy programs for patient education
